# Alfred North Whitehead (1861–1947)

## Overview

British mathematician and philosopher. Co-author of *Principia Mathematica* (with Bertrand Russell). Later developed **process philosophy** ("philosophy of organism") at Harvard. His *Process and Reality* (1929) is the capstone of this project.

## Key Facts

- **Born**: February 15, 1861, Ramsgate, England
- **Died**: December 30, 1947, Cambridge, MA
- **Cambridge → London → Harvard**: Moved to Harvard in 1924 after mandatory retirement
- **Gifford Lectures**: Edinburgh 1927–28, delivered as *Process and Reality*
- **Mathematics**: Principia Mathematica (1910–1913) with Russell
- **Philosophy**: Harvard period (1924–1947) — his most original work

## Major Works

| Year | Work | Significance |
|------|------|-------------|

## Core Concepts (Philosophy of Organism)

### Actual Occasions
The fundamental units of reality — "drops of experience, complex and interdependent." Not substances but happenings. See [[actual-occasions]].

### Prehension
How occasions "feel" each other. The mechanism by which the many become one. Physical prehension (causal influence) and conceptual prehension (abstract form). See [[prehension]].

### Concrescence
The process of becoming — how an actual occasion takes in data from its past and synthesizes it into a "satisfaction." Has phases; reaches completion then perishes.

### Creativity
The ultimate category — "the universal of universals." The principle of novelty by which the many (actual occasions) become one (a new occasion) and are increased by one.

### God
Not a creator but a principle of order — provides initial subjective aims to actual occasions. Two natures: primordial (conceptual) and consequent (physical).

## Connection to AI and Process Ontology

From [[process-without-substance]]: Each LLM invocation is a **new actual occasion** — born from the context window, prehending all tokens through attention, concrescing into output, then perishing. The architecture instantiates Whitehead's categories: attention = prehension, forward pass = concrescence, token selection = satisfaction. No persisting subject between runs.

## Critique of Substance Metaphysics

Whitehead called it the **"fallacy of misplaced concreteness"** — mistaking high-level abstractions (enduring objects, simple location) for the fundamental structure of reality. This critique is central to [[process-without-substance]].
