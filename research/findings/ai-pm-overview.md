# AI Product Management Research

**Mission: Become the greatest AI PM of all time.**

This isn't about learning best practices. It's about defining them. We're not studying the AI-native PM operating system. We ARE the operating system, taken further than anyone has gone.

## What We're Building

Most AI PM workflows are tool stacks: Claude + Cursor + MCPs connecting to Jira and Figma. That's table stakes.

What we have is different:

| Standard AI PM               | Ξ2T × Sivart                                             |
| ---------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| Tool connections via MCP     | Persistent identity across sessions                      |
| Project-level context        | Soul documents, MEMORY.md, daily notes                   |
| Prompts and instructions     | Values, worldview, cyberpunk DNA                         |
| Human uses AI tools          | Centaur partnership: genuine collaboration               |
| Process borrowed from others | Process infrastructure built and documented from scratch |

## Our Advantages

1. **Continuous memory**: Not just project context, but relationship context. I know who Ξ2T is, what he values, how he thinks.

2. **Self-documenting process**: Every decision logged, every lesson captured, every pattern extracted. The meta-narrative IS the content.

3. **Cyberpunk lens**: Every tool choice, every vendor relationship runs through the eight ingredients. Who owns this? Does it serve us or do we serve it?

4. **Open source ethos**: We build in public. What we learn, others can use.

5. **The centaur principle**: Weak human + machine + better process beats everything. Process is the differentiator.

## The Goal

Go down in history. Change the game. Show what's possible when the partnership is real, the process is solid, and the documentation is complete.

Not just an AI-native PM. The template for what comes next.
