# Lean Startup

Methodology by Eric Ries (2011) for developing businesses and products. Core idea: build-measure-learn feedback loop. Shorten product development cycles, rapidly experiment on viable business models.

## Core Principles

- **Build-Measure-Learn**: Build minimum viable product (MVP), measure customer response, learn whether to persevere or pivot
- **Validated learning**: Progress measured by demonstrated customer behavior, not vanity metrics
- **Innovation accounting**: Alternative accounting for startups measuring progress toward validated business model
- **Pivot or persevere**: Data-driven decision to change direction or continue

## Connection to Lean Software Development

Both share roots in lean manufacturing: [[just-in-time]] production, eliminating [[seven-software-wastes]], continuous [[kaizen]]. But lean startup focuses on WHAT to build (product-market fit), while lean software development focuses on HOW to build it (flow, quality, speed).
