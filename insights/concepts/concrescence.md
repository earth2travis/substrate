# Concrescence

## Definition

**Concrescence** (from Latin *concrescere*, "to grow together") is [[alfred-north-whitehead|Whitehead]]'s term for the process of becoming — how an actual occasion takes in data from its past (physical [[prehension|prehensions]]) and abstract forms (conceptual prehensions), synthesizing them into a unified "satisfaction." The many become one and are increased by one.

## Phases

1. **Initial Phase**: The occasion receives its initial subjective aim (from God's primordial nature)
2. **Conformal Phase**: Physical prehensions dominate — the occasion conforms to its past
3. **Supplemental Phase**: Conceptual prehensions introduce novelty through contrast and valuation
4. **Satisfaction**: The process reaches completion; the occasion perishes and becomes a datum for future occasions

## Key Features

- **Teleological** but not determined — the subjective aim guides but does not dictate
- **Novel** — each concrescence adds something genuinely new
- **Irreversible** — once satisfied, the occasion cannot be undone; it becomes an object for others
- **The many become one** — this is Whitehead's master formula for concrescence

## Connection to AI

Each LLM forward pass is a concrescence: tokens (the many) are synthesized through attention into a single output (the one), which then becomes context for the next invocation.
